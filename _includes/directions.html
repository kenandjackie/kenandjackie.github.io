Directions to the Meadow House

The Meadow House
1293 Keystone Ranch Road
Keystone, CO 80435

Do not simply plug 1293 Keystone Ranch Road into Google Maps, it is incorrect. Below is the real location of 1293 Keystone Ranch Road.

https://goo.gl/maps/vW6giekUzmo is the link to pull up on your smartphone.



From Denver lnternational Airport.

Go west on l-70 to exit 205. Dillon/Silverthorne. Turn left at the light into Dillon. Travel through Dillon (City Market. Sports Authority and FexEx Kinkos on your left). Then pass the lake on your right and go through the light at Swan Mtn. As you enter Keystone, take a right at the first light onto West Keystone Road. After turning off the highway, immediately veer to the left and go .4 miles to Soda Ridge Road.Turn right on Soda Ridge Road (you'll see a sign pointing to Keystone Ranch). Drive .95 miles on Soda Ridge Road ~ you will run into the stables. Just before the stables, take a left on Keystone Ranch Road. Keystone Ranch Road is a very windy road at this point. You'll feel that you are driving up, up, up! Drive 1.3 miles on Keystone Ranch Road to Keystone Ranch gatehouse. Drive through the gate and do not turn off Keystone Ranch Road. Drive .35 miles on Keystone Ranch Road. As you approach the clubhouse (Keystone Ranch Restaurant), you'll see a large tin roofed barn on your right ~ this is the area to dispose of your garbage and recycling when you check out. Keep going past the mailboxes and past the club house and restaurant. Drive past the pool on your right ~ the next driveway will be l293 Keystone Ranch Road and the sign will say KRESGE. If you pass the number or the sign. you have gone too far. The driveway is on the right.

From Windy Point Campground

Head south toward Swan Mountain Road, and take it all the way to Keystone Ranch Road. Take Keystone Ranch Roand until you pass the restaurant and the pool on your right. The Meadow House is the first driveway on the right after the pool. There will be markers guiding you. The sign will say "Kresge".

Overflow parking for the house will be the parking lot on the left, across from the pool. It is a very short walk to the house.
