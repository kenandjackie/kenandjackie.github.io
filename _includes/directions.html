From the highway (I-70 West)

Take exit 205 from I-70 W. Turn left onto Blue River Parkway (signs for US-6 E/Dillon). Continue onto US-6 east for 6 miles, and turn right onto Keystone Ranch Road. Take Keystone Ranch Roand until you pass the restaurant and the pool on your right. The Meadow House is the first driveway on the right after the pool. There will be markers guiding you. The sign will say "Kresge".

From Windy Point Campground

Head south toward Swan Mountain Road, and take it all the way to Keystone Ranch Road. Take Keystone Ranch Roand until you pass the restaurant and the pool on your right. The Meadow House is the first driveway on the right after the pool. There will be markers guiding you. The sign will say "Kresge".

Overflow parking for the house will be the parking lot on the left, across from the pool. It is a very short walk to the house.
